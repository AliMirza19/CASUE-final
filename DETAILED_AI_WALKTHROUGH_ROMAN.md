# 🧠 CAUSE AI Cognitive Core: Mukammal Walkthrough (Roman Urdu)

Yeh guide aap ko CAUSE Society ke naye AI features samjhane aur test karne mein madad degi. Her feature ko Roman Urdu mein asaan alfaz mein explain kiya gaya hai.

---

## 1. AI Smart Ticker (Live Dashboard Updates)
**Yeh Kya Hai?** 
Aap ke dashboard ke top per aik chalti hui line (Marquee) nazar aaye gi jo society ki live halat batati hai.

**Test Kaise Karein?**
1. Kisi bhi bare account (Patron, HOD, ya Student Affairs) se login karein.
2. Dashboard ke top per dekhein, aap ko 4-5 mukhtalif messages nazar ayenge.
3. **Logic:** AI peeche se budget, pending events, aur election registration ka data check kerta hai aur khud se "punchy" lines banata hai.
**Fayda:** Leadership ko reports parhne ki zaroorat nahi, sirf ticker dekh ker society ka status pata chal jata hai.

---

## 2. AI Risk Auditor (Event Proposal Audit)
**Yeh Kya Hai?** 
Jab koi student naya event propose kerta hai, to AI foran check kerta hai ke is mein koi "ghapla" ya risk to nahi.

**Test Kaise Karein?**
1. **Student** se login kerke aik naya event create karein (Rs 50,000 budget ke sath).
2. Phir **Patron** ya **HOD** se login karein aur "Manage Events" mein jayein.
3. Event open karein, wahan "AI Risk Assessment" ka section hoga.
4. **Logic:** AI check kerta hai ke kya budget kaafi zyada hai? Kya dates takra rahi hain? Wahan AI flags show kerr dega (Low, Medium, ya High risk).
**Fayda:** Corruption aur faltu kharchon ka pehle hi pata chal jata hai.

---

## 3. AI Volunteer Matching (Smart Team Building)
**Yeh Kya Hai?** 
Kise event ke liye kon sa volunteer best hai, yeh AI batata hai.

**Test Kaise Karein?**
1. **Volunteer Coordinator (VC)** dashboard per jayein.
2. "Volunteer Requests" open karein.
3. Wahan her volunteer ke sath **"AI Match Score"** ya aik button hoga jo unhein rank kerr raha hoga.
4. **Logic:** AI student ki profile se un ki skills (e.g. Photoshop, PHP) uthata hai aur event ki requirement se match kerta hai.
**Fayda:** Galat kaam ke liye galat banda select nahi hoga, kaam hamesha expert banda karega.

---

## 4. AI Manifesto Optimizer (Candidate Registration)
**Yeh Kya Hai?** 
Students ko election ke liye professional manifesto likhne mein madad kerta hai.

**Test Kaise Karein?**
1. **Election Timeline** set karein (Registration open ho).
2. **Student** account se "Register as Candidate" per jayein.
3. "Manifesto" box mein kuch tora sa likhen (e.g. "Main society ko upar le jaonga").
4. **"AI Optimize"** button per click karein.
5. **Logic:** AI Groq/Llama model use kerte huye manifesto ko professional, catchy aur inspiring bana dega.
**Fayda:** Students ki leadership quality barhti hai aur elections professional lagte hain.

---

## 5. AI Creative Assistant (Graphic Designer Support)
**Yeh Kya Hai?** 
Graphic Designer ko brainstorming mein help kerta hai.

**Test Kaise Karein?**
1. **Graphic Designer (GD)** dashboard per jayein.
2. Koi bhi assignment ya request open karein.
3. Wahan AI ne "Theme Persona" di hogi (e.g., "Cyberpunk for Gaming Event").
4. **Logic:** AI event ke title aur desc ko parh ker bata-ta hai ke color matching kya honi chahiye aur vibe kaisi ho.
**Fayda:** Designing ka kaam 50% asaan ho jata hai kyunke ideas AI de raha hota hai.

---

## 6. AI Decision Support (Market Rate Verification)
**Yeh Kya Hai?** 
Budget approve karte waqt AI batata hai ke "PKR 500 per Certificate" mehnga hai ya sasta.

**Test Kaise Karein?**
1. **HOD** dashboard per event review karein.
2. Budget item per mouse le jayein ya neechay "Decision Support" card dekhein.
3. **Logic:** AI ke pass market rates ka context hai. Agar rate unusual ho to woh HOD ko warn kerr deta hai.
**Fayda:** Society ka aik aik rupay sahi jagah kharch hota hai.

---

## 7. AI Meeting Minutes (Chat Summarizer)
**Yeh Kya Hai?** 
Chat history ko parh ker uska nichor (summary) nikaalna.

**Test Kaise Karein?**
1. **HOD** aur **Patron** ki chat open karein.
2. Wahan thori baatein karein ke "Event date badal do", "Budget kam kero".
3. "Generate Action Items" per click karein.
4. **Logic:** AI sari chat parh ker sirf "kaam ki baatein" points mein nikal ker deta hai.
**Fayda:** Pura chat history parhne ki zaroorat nahi paregi.
